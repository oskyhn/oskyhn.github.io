abstract: "We jointly combine image classification and image denoising to enhance human perception of noisy images captured by edge devices, like low-light security cameras. We alter a Neural Architecture Search (NAS) method to search for integrated models optimizing for target latency, classification accuracy, and denoising performance. The NAS architectures outperform manually designed alternatives in both denoising and classification."
