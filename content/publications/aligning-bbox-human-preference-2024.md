abstract: "Previous work shows that humans tend to prefer large bounding boxes over small bounding boxes with the same IoU. We evaluated three commonly used object detectors through a user study (N=123) and found that humans prefer object detections that are upscaled with factors of 1.5 or 2. We propose an asymmetric bounding box regression loss that encourages large over small predicted bounding boxes, showing that detectors fine-tuned with this loss are better aligned with human preference."
