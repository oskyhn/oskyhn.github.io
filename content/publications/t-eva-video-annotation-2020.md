abstract: "We propose a time-efficient video annotation method using spatio-temporal feature similarity and t-SNE dimensionality reduction to speed up the annotation process massively. The method places the same actions from different videos near each other in two-dimensional space based on feature similarity to help annotators group-label video clips. Evaluated on ActivityNet and Sports-1M datasets."
