abstract: "We challenge the assumption that convolutional neural networks maintain translation invariance. We demonstrate that CNNs can and will exploit the absolute spatial location by learning filters that respond exclusively to particular absolute locations by exploiting image boundary effects. We propose a solution to improve translation invariance, which proves beneficial for small datasets across image classification, patch matching, and video classification tasks."
