abstract: "We examine how the Intersection over Union (IoU) score used to evaluate object detector localization quality compares to human perception. Through a survey with more than 70 participants, we found that humans might not consider localization errors with the exact same IoU score as equal. The work concludes that relying on IoU scores alone to evaluate localization errors might not be sufficient."
