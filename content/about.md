I'm the Technical Data and AI Lead at Haga Teaching Hospital, where I focus on developing AI-driven solutions for healthcare and imaging. I'm also a Guest Researcher at Technische Universiteit Delft, where I explore the intersection of AI, computer vision and data-efficient learning methods.

## Background

Previously, I worked at Bosch Security System, where I designed and led AI projects for security camera systems. My PhD research at TU Delft focused on locality in space and time for data-efficient visual recognition, providing insights into optimizing AI for resource-constrained environments.

I've been actively involved in bridging industry and academia, mentoring students and collaborating with leading researchers on AI applications across various domains. My expertise spans deep learning, computer vision, and AI for healthcare, and I've contributed to several high-impact research publications.

## Focus

With a passion for AI's transformative potential in healthcare, I continue to explore how machine learning can improve hospital workflows, from automating clinical processes to improving diagnostic accuracy and patient care.
